Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2025

1. No personal-data collection

All compression happens locally in your browser using WebAssembly. ImgSqueeze does not upload, store, log or access your images or their metadata.

We set no cookies except an optional preference cookie (theme=dark|light) if you toggle dark mode.

2. Optional analytics

If enabled, we use Plausible Analytics with IP anonymisation and no cookies. You can opt-out at any time via the link in the site footer.

3. Contact

Email: hello@imgsqueeze.com

4. Your rights

Because no personal data is processed, GDPR access and erasure rights generally do not apply. If you believe data was collected in error, contact us.

5. Changes

We may update this notice from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted within the app.
